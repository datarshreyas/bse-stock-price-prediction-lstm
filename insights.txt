Key Insights:

- LSTM captures overall stock price trends but struggles with short-term volatility
- Patterns learned from one stock do not fully transfer to another
- Differences in scale, volatility, and fundamentals impact predictions

Business Interpretation:

- Historical price data alone is not sufficient for reliable forecasting
- Cross-stock modelling highlights the need for external data (market, sector, news)
- Time-series models are better suited for trend analysis than precise prediction
